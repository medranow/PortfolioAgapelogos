About me

I am Walter Medrano from Guatemala. Over the past 8 years, I have focused my efforts on studying and creating with light, movement, and sound.

I attended John Brown University in AR, where I earned a degree in digital media arts. Later, I pursued an MA in international relations at Universidad Francisco Marroquin.

My passion for cinema has been influenced by ‘los tres amigos’: Guillermo del Toro, Alejandro Iñárritu and Alfonso Cuarón. I am pumped to tell your story!
